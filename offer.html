QUESTIONS

Before you ask

Is it actually free?
Yes. 14 days, full dashboard access, daily analysis, weekly reports. No credit card, no commitment. After 14 days, you can continue with a monthly plan or simply walk away with all the insights you gained.
What do I need to install?
A lightweight tracking snippet added to your site — similar to Google Analytics but privacy-first. No cookies, fully GDPR-compliant, won't slow down your site. Takes 5 minutes, and we can walk you through it.
What happens after 14 days?
You choose. If the intelligence is valuable and you want ongoing monitoring, reports, and recommendations, we discuss a plan that fits your business. If not, the dashboard simply stops updating. No pressure.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We use privacy-first tracking — no cookies, no personally identifiable information collected. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. You can request full deletion at any time.
What kind of businesses is this for?
Any business with a website. Ecommerce stores, SaaS products, service businesses, local businesses, agencies. If your site gets traffic, we can find the conversion gaps, track your AI search visibility, and tell you exactly what to improve.
How is this different from Google Analytics?
Google Analytics shows you numbers. We show you what to do about them. You get specific, prioritized recommendations every day — not just charts. Plus we track AI referral traffic (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) that GA4 misses entirely.
What kind of recommendations will I get?
Things like: "68% of visitors leave your homepage without scrolling — move your CTA above the fold" or "Your pricing page loads in 4.2 seconds — compress these 3 images to cut load time in half." Specific, actionable, ranked by impact.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
No. The dashboard is designed for business owners, not developers. Every recommendation is written in plain English with clear next steps. If something needs a developer, we'll tell you exactly what to ask them to do.
