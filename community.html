Welcome to Tauranga

New to the Bay of Plenty? Here's your step-by-step guide to settling in as a Muslim in Tauranga.

1

Airport Pickup

Need a ride from Auckland or Tauranga airport? Contact the mosque and we'll arrange a free pickup from a community member.

2

Temporary Accommodation

We have community members who offer guest rooms for new arrivals (up to 2 weeks). Check the Housing tab or contact the mosque.

3

Visit the Mosque

Come for any prayer or Friday Jumu'ah. Introduce yourself to the Imam or any committee member. You'll be welcomed warmly, inshaAllah.

4

Join the WhatsApp Group

Our community WhatsApp group is where everything happens — announcements, events, questions, and help requests. Ask at the mosque for the link.

5

Find Halal Food

Check the Halal Food Guide for butchers, restaurants, and grocery stores near you.

6

Enrol Your Kids

Local schools: Tauranga Primary, Tauranga Intermediate, Otumoetai College. The mosque runs Quran and Arabic classes on weekends for kids aged 5-14.

7

Set Up Essentials

Bank account (ANZ, ASB, BNZ all nearby), IRD number, GP enrollment. Check our Professionals Directory for Muslim-friendly services.

8

Get Involved

Join Badir (youth builders), volunteer at the mosque, list your business in the directory, or offer your skills. This is your community now.

🕌 When a Death Occurs

  1. Contact the mosque committee immediately (WhatsApp or phone)
  2. The committee will coordinate with the funeral home
  3. Ghusl (ritual washing) will be arranged by trained community members
  4. Kafan (shrouding) is provided by the mosque
  5. Janazah prayer will be announced to the community
  6. Burial is arranged at the nearest Muslim section of a local cemetery
  7. Community support: meals, childcare, and companionship for the family

❤ Crisis Support

If you or someone you know is struggling, these services are confidential and free:

Need to talk? Call or text 1737 (24/7)
Domestic violence: Women's Refuge 0800 733 843
Family support: Contact the mosque Imam (confidential)
Financial hardship: Zakat fund available — speak to committee

📖 Islamic Guidance

The mosque and community are here for you in any situation:

Shahada: Want to take your shahada? Contact the Imam
Marriage (Nikah): Imam can officiate — book in advance
Islamic counselling: Available through mosque referral
Reversion support: Buddy system for new Muslims
