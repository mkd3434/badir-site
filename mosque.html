About the Mosque

A home for every Muslim in Tauranga.

The Tauranga Mosque serves as the spiritual and community heart for Muslims in the Bay of Plenty region. A place where families gather, youth grow, and the community builds together.

Whether you're new to Tauranga, a long-time local, or just visiting — you're welcome here. We offer daily prayers, educational programs, youth initiatives, and a warm community that feels like family.

Our vision goes beyond worship. We're building a community that creates — through the Badir builder initiative, weekend workshops, and programs that turn inspiration into action.

Support the Mosque

Every dollar is sadaqah jariyah.

Your donations keep the mosque running, fund youth programs, and build the community. Every contribution counts.

The Tauranga Mosque runs entirely on community donations. From electricity to educational materials, your support keeps the doors open for every prayer, every class, and every community gathering.

Your contribution directly funds:

Mosque operations — utilities, maintenance, cleaning
Youth programs — Badir builder community, youth circles
Education — Quran classes, Islamic studies materials
Community events — iftars, Eid celebrations, family days
Outreach — new Muslim support, dawah activities
