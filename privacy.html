Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 April 2026

Badir Studio ("we", "us", "our") operates badir.studio. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

1. What we collect

  • Waitlist signups: Email address
  • Project discovery survey: Name, email, role, business name, website, project details, budget range, and referral source
  • Brand readiness scorecard: Assessment answers and scores (no personal data unless you provide your email)
  • Analytics: Page views, referrer URL, browser type, and country — via Vercel Web Analytics (no cookies, no personal identifiers)

2. How we use your data

  • To respond to your project inquiry within 48 hours
  • To send a confirmation email when you join the waitlist or submit a survey
  • To notify our team of new signups and submissions
  • To understand which pages are visited and improve the site

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-party services

  • Vercel (hosting + serverless functions + analytics) — Privacy Policy
  • Resend (transactional email) — Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts (font delivery) — Privacy Policy

4. Data storage and security

Form submissions are processed by Vercel serverless functions and delivered to us via Resend email. We do not store your data in a database. Submissions are logged in Vercel's function logs (retained for 1 hour on the free plan) and in our email inbox.

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We use security headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options) to protect the site.

5. Your rights (NZ Privacy Act 2020)

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@badir.studio.

6. Cookies

We do not use cookies. Vercel Web Analytics is cookie-free and does not track individual users.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
