Proprietary Method

The CORE Framework

Every brand we build starts here. Four dimensions that turn a vague idea into a clear, differentiated brand.

C

Core Purpose

Why does this exist beyond making money? The purpose that gets you out of bed and makes customers care. Not a mission statement — a genuine reason.

O

Origin Story

What brought you here? The personal or market insight that sparked this idea. Origin stories create emotional connection that features never can.

R

Resonance Points

What specifically connects with your audience? Not what you think they want — what they actually say, feel, and search for. Grounded in real signals.

E

Expression System

Voice, visuals, messaging, design system. How the brand shows up everywhere — website, social, emails, product. Consistent, recognizable, yours.

Common Questions

The objections we hear most.

Real questions from real builders. No corporate non-answers.

"But I just need a website — do I really need strategy?"

+

A website without strategy is a billboard in the desert. Before writing a single line of code, you need to know: Who is your customer? What do they care about? How are you different from the 10 other people offering the same thing? Strategy answers these questions. The website then becomes the execution of those answers — not a guess.

"Is strategy worth it for a side project or early idea?"

+

Especially for early ideas. The earlier you validate, the less time you waste building the wrong thing. A Strategy Sprint takes 48 hours. Building the wrong MVP costs months and far more. Strategy is the cheapest insurance you can buy against building something nobody wants.

"What if I already have a brand?"

+

Then we audit what you have. Most businesses have a logo and colours but no actual brand strategy — no positioning, no voice guidelines, no competitor differentiation, no ICP profile. We run a Brand Audit to identify gaps, then fill them. If your brand is genuinely strong, we skip to Build.
